Minimal flat chart for reports — bar (spending trend) or donut (category breakdown). Always pair with a textual summary/legend for accessibility; never 3D, gradient-filled, or animated per the brief.

```jsx
<Chart type="donut" data={[{label:'بقالة',value:1200,color:'var(--color-primary)'},{label:'مواصلات',value:400,color:'var(--color-info)'}]} />
```
